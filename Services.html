Service Offering

Moses Silinda Inc. aim to build a professional and understanding relationship with our clients to help them achieve their goals.

We assist our clients to avoid legal challenges whilst operating in a hostile environment, so that they can focus on the success and growth of their businesses.

Our Attorneys understand their clients' strategic objectives as well as their risk management profiles, and will assist and offer advise accordingly.

We believe that placing your challenges with us, your legal matters will be in good hands and that having a good Attorney on your side will help you through the many pit-falls that you could experience.

We take the ‘sting’ out of having to understand all the different practices of law.

Our team have the ability to handle both small and large complex cases, and we strive to serve our clients to the best of our ability and to your total satisfaction.
